Texture Screen

One of the most important things is the texture, in furniture industry we work with more than one type os material, you can have the MDF and MDP, in fact we have more than two material but this two is the most used and maybe is you use another type maybe they need mor atributes in the system, so you don’t used the system to management. Beside of Material we have the texture, when the material have a wood texture that means they need to be treated completely different, when you start a project with this type of material you need to think about the wood texture direction, and this interfere in cut process, in general, wood texture have a big loss because in the process of optimization the direction can make a lot of pieces on the other hand the solid textures they can be cut without direction because is a solid texture and give us a better optimization so the data based have a binary column named “Veio”, that help the system give us this information.
